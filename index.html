About Us

M & M's Civil Engineering Solutions Pvt. Ltd. is one of fastest growing and highly reputed firms in the field of civil engineering.Since its inception in 1996 as Nishith and Associates, our company has remained committed to assisting individuals in bringing their visions to life. We offer unparalleled architectural solutions to those embarking on the pursuit of their dream homes. In 2002, we transitioned to a partnership and established Vaastuniketan in Puthiyangadi, Calicut. Later, in 2016, we rebranded as an independent firm called M and M Civil Engineering Solutions, located in West Hill Chungam. With a remarkable track record of over 2500+ successfully completed designs and executions in South India, we specialize in Traditional architecture as well as renovation and facelift projects.

We have expertise in providing sustainable solutions in the execution of large and complex urban and industrial infrastructure projects We specialize in a wide range of civil engineering services, including infrastructure design, construction management, site development, and project planning.
